What is Korrect?

Korrect is a user feedback management tool where your users can give you feedback on your product or upvote on feature requests. It is the korrect way to get feedback from your users and improve your product. Korrect helps you prioritise and remove features that are not working as expected with your users. You can be transparent to your users about what you are working on and reduce the time spent responding to feature requests. Korrect, being the best feedback tool power hundreds of product guys and saves their time and energy.

How do I get started?

You can register here for free. After registering, you can immediately start creating your first boards and implementing them on your website or in your tool.

What is feature voting?

Feature voting is the ability to have your users vote on features they would like to use. It’s a great way to know what to build next and reduce the risk of losing customers.

What are user feedback boards?

User feedback boards are a great way to organise feedback from your users. You can segregate and collect feedback from your users in different categories with boards. Some of the most most used feedback boards could be:

  • Feature requests
  • Bug reports
  • Support tickets
  • Internal feedback

Is Korrect free?

Yes, Korrect is free to use forever. If you need unlimited usage in terms of boards, users etc, you can consider switching to 'Pro' version.

Where is Korrect hosted?

Korrect's servers are hosted within the EU. Our backup servers, clusters, blob storage, and others included.

Is Korrect GDPR compliant?

Yes. Korrect is GDPR compliant. You can read more about GDPR compliance in our GDPR policy.

Do I need a Credit Card to try?

No. You can get started with Korrect in a matter of minutes without any credit card details.
